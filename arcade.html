Taito HEY




Tokyo Arcade

Arcade Stop!

On the Chuo Dori, one of the main roads in Akihabara, is Hirose Entertainment Yard or just “HEY”; a fairly large multi-story arcade that actually looks rather unremarkable. It’s also not the only large arcade in Akihabara either, as there are a few Club Segas and Taito Stations as well. It is, however, in my opinion the best.

Run by Taito, hence why it is often called Taito Hey, it is split into two main areas; classic shoot-em-ups and fighting games. The fighting floor features all the current modern beat-em-ups but also holds host to classic fighters too. What’s nice about this is that not only are the contemporary games played regularly but also the classic ones too. The communities built around the older games still thrive as they once did and that’s something that is entirely fascinating to see and participate in.
